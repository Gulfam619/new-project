Apartment operating options

Guarantee Program

The apartment owner hands over his property to a management company, which takes care of its exploitation and operation, while we guarantee the owner a paid income of 5% of the acquisition value of the property excluding VAT for 2 years. The owner receives this income on an agreed date to his bank account. With this program, the owner does not pay a management fee, media and energy for 2 years and has a carefree investment. After the 24-month period, the owner, by mutual agreement, extends the lease agreement through the Guarantee program in the form of an amendment to the lease agreement or automatically switches to the 65/35 Profitability Program. The apartment owner can use his apartment for his needs for 3 weeks a year.

Profitability 65/35 Program

The apartment owner will hand over their property to a management company, which will take care of its exploitation and operation, while the owner will be paid a yield depending on the actual utilization of the project in the ratio of 65/35. 65% of the yield value will be paid to the owner into a bank account at a specified frequency. The remaining 35% remains with the management company (from this amount it will pay for marketing activities, cleaning services, etc.). In this program, the apartment owner pays an advance payment for property management and electricity consumption. We estimate the annual yield between 6 and 10%.The apartment owner can use his apartment 3 weeks a year.

Individual Program

The apartment owner can furnish his apartment according to his wishes, he can use the services of the apartment hotel. He can use the apartment whenever he sees fit. The apartment owner concludes a contract on joining the management of the building and makes regular advance payments for the apartment to the management company.
