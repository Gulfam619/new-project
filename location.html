A PLACE WHERE EVERY BREATH GIVES STRENGTH

Donovaly is located in the Low Tatras National Park NAPANT at an altitude of 960 m above sea level, on the border of the Veľká Fatra and Starohorské vrchy mountains, between Ružomberok (27 km away) and Banská Bystrica (25 km away). Although Donovaly has only 248 permanent residents, occasional tourists and regular visitors love this mountain village. No wonder, because it is surrounded by nature on all sides. Here you will find postcard-beautiful scenery and air that fills you with peace and vitality with every breath.

Even though you will find all the comforts of home in your apartment, the beautiful Tatra nature will not allow you to stay inside for long. There are up to 14 cycling routes and more than 10 hiking trails in the area. Did you come lightly? No problem. You can rent electric scooters, bicycles and electric bicycles directly in the village. Adrenaline enthusiasts can try paragliding, a rope park, a mountain bobsleigh track, mountain climbing or aerial activities. Nearby Hrabovo, Krpáčovo and Turček are renowned fishing locations.

CHILDREN FORGET

WHAT BORING IS

Little ones can be extremely demanding when it comes to entertainment, so it's good that during your stay at MODENA APARTMENTS Donovaly you can offer them a whole range of action-packed activities. Family attractions such as Donovalkovo, Habakuky, FUN Arena, Myšiačik's Children's Park or Bobsleigh will "excite" even the most restless little ones. Donovalkovo regularly organizes children's themed events such as Frozen Day, Disney Day, Night in Donovalkovo, Race Day or Carnival Day. Children will experience everything here, but not boredom.
